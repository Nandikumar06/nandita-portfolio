Energy Monitoring

Smart Energy Monitoring System

Tracks electricity usage, estimates consumption patterns, and improves energy efficiency using a smart monitoring approach.

Problem

Users often do not have a clear view of appliance-level or total electricity consumption, which leads to higher power wastage and inefficient usage.

Solution

Built a system concept to monitor energy usage in real time, helping users understand power consumption and make better energy-saving decisions.

Outcome

Created a practical smart-energy idea that supports awareness, efficiency, and better control over electricity usage.

Retail Automation

Smart Shopping Trolley

A supermarket trolley concept that simplifies product tracking and billing to reduce queue time and improve the shopping experience.

Problem

Traditional billing systems create long checkout queues and increase the time customers spend waiting after shopping.

Solution

Environmental Sensing

Multi-Wavelength Optical Microplastic Detection

An optical sensing concept that uses multiple wavelengths of light to improve the detection of microplastics for environmental monitoring.

Problem

Microplastic pollution is difficult to detect accurately using basic inspection methods, especially when fast and scalable monitoring is needed.

Solution

Designed a sensing approach based on multi-wavelength optical analysis to identify microplastic presence more effectively.

Outcome

This project highlights an innovative environmental application of electronics and optical sensing for sustainability-focused monitoring.
